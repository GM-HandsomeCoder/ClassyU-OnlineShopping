ClassyÜ Gel Ink Pens are made with an ink mechanism for smoother writing which also prevents the ink from leaking. They are designed for

smooth and comfortable writing, making it perfect for everyday use.




Our iconic ClassyÜ Gel Ink Pens feature a special mechanism that prevents leakage and backflow of ink, which allows for smooth and precise writing and reduces smudging.

The water-based ink does not easily smear or bleed through paper, making it an ideal choice for writing and art. Designed to be sleek and simple, the pen sports a conveniently

retractable frosted body. Available in a wide variety of colours and compatible with the gel ink pen refills. (Sold separately)




 We redesigned our pens to make them compatible with all gel ink refills in order to reduce waste. This also allows you to customize your

own pen according to your preference in color, body, ink, and thickness.
